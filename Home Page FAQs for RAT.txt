9. How much time does it take to develop a game?
Development timeline depends on scope, platform, and complexity. On average:
    • Simple 2D casual games: 3–6 months
    • Mid-level mobile/PC games (with multiplayer, 3D assets, or moderate features): 6–12 months
    • Advanced AAA-style or large-scale multiplayer games: 12–24+ months
    • Ongoing live-service games: Continuous updates & support beyond initial launch
Factors influencing timeline:
    • Game genre & mechanics
    • Art & animation complexity
    • Number of platforms (mobile, PC, console, VR/AR)
    • Multiplayer vs. single-player
    • Testing & quality assurance requirements
We always start with a clear project roadmap, covering phases, milestones, and deliverables.
