FAQs
When should we choose custom e-commerce development over Shopify or WooCommerce?
Custom development is ideal when you need advanced workflows, deep ERP/CRM integrations, multi-vendor logic, global scaling, or long-term flexibility that off-the-shelf platforms can’t support without costly workarounds.
Can you migrate our existing e-commerce platform without losing SEO or revenue?
Yes. We follow a zero-downtime migration strategy with data synchronization, URL preservation, parallel environments, and extensive testing to protect rankings and transactions.
How do you ensure performance during peak traffic and flash sales?
We use cloud auto-scaling, CDN integration, load balancing, optimized caching, and performance profiling to ensure stability during traffic spikes.
Who owns the source code and intellectual property?
You retain full ownership of the source code, data, integrations, and IP. We provide complete documentation and handover.
How do you prevent another re-platforming in 1–2 years?
We design modular, API-driven architectures that support feature expansion, integrations, and scaling without rebuilding the core system.
Can you integrate ERP, CRM, POS, and warehouse systems?
Yes. We specialize in enterprise integrations with SAP, Salesforce, NetSuite, Odoo, custom ERPs, and logistics platforms.
What factors influence the cost of custom e-commerce development?
Cost depends on architecture complexity, integrations, feature depth, security requirements, scalability needs, and timeline—not just UI or catalog size.
Do you provide post-launch support and optimization?
Yes. We offer SLA-based maintenance, monitoring, security updates, performance tuning, and continuous feature enhancements.
Can you support global expansion with multi-language and multi-currency features?
Absolutely. We implement localization, regional tax rules, global payments, and geo-optimized experiences to support international growth.
