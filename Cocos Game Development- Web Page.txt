FAQs
Why should I choose Cocos Creator for my game project?
Cocos Creator offers excellent performance, cross-platform deployment, open-source flexibility, and fast development, which is ideal for startups and enterprises seeking efficient game delivery.
Can you migrate an existing Unity or native game app to Cocos?
Definitely! Our mobile game development skills include migrating your existing Unity or native game app to Cocos efficiently. For that, we deploy steps like:
    • Asset Optimization
    • Codebase Re-architecture
    • Performance Tuning
    • Platform Compatibility
How long does it typically take to build a full Cocos game?
It usually takes around 8-24 weeks to develop a Cocos game, depending on the complexity, features, and platform targets of your project. But to get a more accurate deadline estimation, share your project details with our enterprising team now.
How do you monetize Cocos games?
We add in-app purchases, ad networks, and reward systems through SDKs and plugins to maximize revenue opportunities.
What engagement models do you offer — Fixed Cost or Dedicated Hiring?
We provide both Fixed Cost and Dedicated Hiring solutions depending on your project’s scope, timeline, and budget flexibility. When you assign us a fixed project scope, we work on the fixed cost model. But when your project comes with evolving scopes, we make sure that you can hire game developers under the Dedicated engagement model. 
How do you manage milestones, communication, and project tracking with clients?
Our game development establishment follows a transparent, milestone-driven, and agile communication approach to ensure that clients stay updated throughout every stage of the project. To do so, we observe steps like:
    • Define clear milestones with deliverables, timelines, and KPIs.
    • Use Jira, Trello, or ClickUp for task tracking and progress visibility.
    • Conduct weekly sprint reviews and daily updates via Slack or Skype.
    • Assign a dedicated Project Manager as the single point of contact.
    • Share real-time dashboards and reports for client transparency.
    • Provide Git/Bitbucket access for version control and live monitoring.
    • Offer staging environments for real-time testing and approvals.
Can my Cocos game scale as user traffic increases?
We design modular game architecture and integrate scalable backend systems to ensure your Cocos-powered game runs smoothly, even with growing player bases.
